Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026 | Omnidesq LLC, Milwaukee, Wisconsin

1. Who We Are

Omnidesq LLC ("Omnidesq," "we," "us," or "our") is a technology company based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin that provides AI-powered voice receptionist services for local businesses. We can be reached at abner@omnidesq.com or +1 (414) 269-3678.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our AI receptionist service (Aria or Jessica), we may collect:

  • Your name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Appointment details (date, time, service type)
  • Call recordings and transcripts for quality assurance
  • Information submitted through our website contact form

We collect this information solely to provide appointment booking and confirmation services on behalf of our business clients.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Book and confirm appointments on behalf of the business you contacted
  • Send appointment confirmations and reminders via email and SMS
  • Notify the business of new or modified bookings
  • Improve our AI receptionist service quality

4. SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and requesting an appointment through our AI receptionist, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your appointment (confirmation, reminders, changes). Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

We do not sell your phone number to third parties. SMS messages are sent only for appointment-related communications.

5. Information Sharing

We share your information only with the specific business whose AI receptionist you contacted (for the purpose of fulfilling your appointment). We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with unrelated third parties.

We use the following service providers to operate our service: Vapi AI (voice AI), Google Calendar (appointment management), Twilio (SMS communications), and Railway (server infrastructure).

6. Data Retention

We retain appointment and contact information for up to 12 months following your last interaction with our service. Call recordings are retained for up to 90 days. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting abner@omnidesq.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal information we hold about you, correct inaccurate information, request deletion of your data, and opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at abner@omnidesq.com.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission, secure server infrastructure, and limited access to personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
